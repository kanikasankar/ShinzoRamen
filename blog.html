How Authentic Japanese Ramen Is Made

Posted on January 2025

Authentic Japanese ramen begins with patience. From slow-simmered broth cooked for over 12 hours to fresh, handmade noodles, every bowl is crafted with care. At Shinzo Ramen in Chennai, we follow traditional techniques inspired by Tokyo ramen shops.

Our Tonkotsu broth starts with premium pork bones gently simmered to extract deep flavor and richness. The noodles are made in-house daily to achieve the perfect chew and texture.

This dedication to tradition is what makes authentic ramen truly comforting.
